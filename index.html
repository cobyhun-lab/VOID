v6.2 — CURRENT
🌀 Orbital weapons buffed — spin faster, orbit further out. Gravity pull drags enemies in.
💥 Pulse weapons added — Shockwave Emitter & Nova Pulse Cannon push enemies (and bullets) away with a visible blast ring.
⚡ Dual weapon slots — buy Dual Weapon Array to equip a second weapon that fires independently.
🖥 New menu flow — splash screen leads to main menu. Settings moved to its own screen.
⚙ Settings screen — music & SFX volume sliders, damage numbers toggle.
🛸 Cylon Raider shape fixed — raiders now draw as a distinct swept crescent with a red eye slit.
💣 Mine layer fixed — mines now drop correctly every 1.8 seconds.
v6.1 — ORBITAL OVERHAUL
🌀 Orbital weapons — Gravity Ring MK1/2 and Singularity Field orbit your ship and crush enemies on contact.
🔄 Shop reroll — spend Tibanna to reroll the shop. Cost increases each reroll, resets per sector.

🔴 Veteran fleet (wave 15+) — faster, harder orange variants with sub-boss escort waves.
🟣 Corrupted enemies (wave 20+) — purple units that split into two shards on death.
🏪 Shop restock (wave 15+) — previously sold items return at 2.5× cost.
📡 Tactical Upgrade — repeatable wave-scaling hull & damage boost unlocks at wave 15.
v5.x — WEAPONS & SHOP
⚔ Weapon audit — all weapons given distinct mechanical roles. Redundant duplicates removed.

🎵 Boss music — aggressive 155 BPM track kicks in on boss waves, returns to normal on clear.
🚀 Ship skins — choose Viper MK II, X-Wing, or Raptor Scout from the main menu.
